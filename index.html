About Us

We carried many emotions into our commitment, which can be felt in the air, as our guests say. The owners and the staff welcome you with warm and friendly attitude. Montenegrin Inn can accommodate 90 people. Whether is business, pleasure or both, Montenegrin Inn is the perfect place for your next vacation. Montenegrin Inn offers double rooms, double rooms with auxiliary bad and four beds studio. The rooms have TV, air-condition, WiFi internet, bathroom. Our guests can relax and enjoy our restaurant, which has capacity of 80 guests, with nice and warm atmosphere.

Due to Montenegrin being a private establishment, the payment methods accepted are cash or a payment to the bank account of the owner.

In august 2004 we opened, for overnight stays, the newly built object Montenegrin Inn, situated in the center of Ohrid, on the main boulevard. What we were looking for so far as guests, we tried to offer to others: spacious, comfortable rooms, sanitary set like they have never before been used, relaxation, nonchalance, intimacy and reasonable rates. You will enjoy the convenient location near to the historic sites, market, downtown to the lake.
Great appreciation to our first guests (from over 20 countries and our country). They gave us motivation to overcome the beginners self consciousness and gain confidence. Also appreciation to all our guests, coincidental or planned, because they are becoming part of our living, not only as names in the guests-book, but as people we remember and who remembers us.
When you stay with us you have Ohrid on your fingertips, you feel its pulse and you forever fall in love with it. All CURRENT PROMOTIONS in Montenegrin Inn can be found on our Facebook fun page.
